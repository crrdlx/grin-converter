How the Website or App Works

By visiting the website or using the app, the converter first gathers the current bitcoin price. The price is loaded into the appropriate field on the form. When a user enters a number into any of the other fields on the form, a javascript script simply converts that current price into the appropriate bitcoin units, and displays those units in the appropriate form fields. This calculation is done entirely locally—either on the computer, tablet, or phone. No information is sent, such as how much bitcoin was entered, anywhere and no information is collected.
